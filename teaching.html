Q-Teams WiSe 21. Organoids: a new window into disease and development

A research-based course where students use state-of-the-art tools to analyze single-cell RNA sequencing datasets and explore organoids stemming from different tissues, and/or within a disease state. The course is part of the Q-Teams program at the Humboldt University. Co-teaching with Tancredi Pentimalli.

Q-Teams SoSe 21. Immune system in breast cancer: friend or foe?

A research-based course where students used state-of-the-art tools to analyze single-cell RNA sequencing datasets in order to study the biology of the immune system and breast cancer. The course was part of the Q-Teams program at the Humboldt University. Co-teaching with Tancredi Pentimalli.
