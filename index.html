I have a solid quantitative training in natural sciences, obtained my PhD in 2012 and been working in research since. My education was primarily on theoretical physics, but apart from mathematical and statistical experience, I have been developing software and data analysis skills.

In 2015 I transited from theoretical physics to systems biology, and I'm currently working in the Max Delbrück Center for Molecular Medicine in Berlin. My work involves data preparation and analysis of high-throughput sequencing data, as well as performing statistical and computational modelling.
